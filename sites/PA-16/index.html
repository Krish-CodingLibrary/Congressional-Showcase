AgriTrader

AgriTrader is an AI-powered agricultural marketplace and price-forecasting platform built for local farmers. The app gives farmers real-time crop price data, ML-based sell-time recommendations, and a peer-to-peer marketplace to buy and sell crops directly — all from their phone, in the field.

The core problem it solves: small-scale farmers often lack access to the same market intelligence that large agricultural operations take for granted. AgriTrader levels that playing field by combining live commodity data, trained machine learning models for corn, soybeans, and wheat, and a clean mobile-first UI that feels approachable to anyone — no finance background required.

The ML models are trained on real Pennsylvania agricultural price data and served through a Python/Flask backend. Predictions come back as confidence-rated buy/hold/sell signals, visualized with animated charts on the forecast screen.
